Download students as a csv

To do this, first open the spreadsheet with the students and click on File→Download.
After that select the Comma Separated Values option.
Then, upload it below and a new csv will be downloaded as pods.csv that has the pod groups.
Make sure that the headers in your spreadsheet are in the same order as the ones here.
If you need the order to be changed, email Milind here .




Upload pod groups csv into Google Sheets

Open a new spreadsheet.
Then select File→Import.
Now click on Upload and select the csv file with the pod groups.
